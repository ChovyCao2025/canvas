# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Marketing Canvas contributors are expected to keep discussions professional,
specific, and welcoming. We value direct technical feedback, but not personal
attacks, harassment, or behavior that makes collaboration unsafe.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use clear, respectful language.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence and tests.
- Keep reviews focused on the change, the requirements, and the user impact.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them promptly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect them.
- Using community spaces to share secrets, credentials, or customer data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or ban
contributors who violate this Code of Conduct. Enforcement decisions should be
proportionate to the impact and documented when practical.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the security or maintainer contact path listed
in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Include links, screenshots, timestamps, and any
prior moderation context when available.
